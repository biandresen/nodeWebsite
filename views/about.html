My Story!

I was born on a planet called Mars in the year 432 BC. My parents, who were both scientists, decided to send me to Earth to study and retrieve knowledge. I learned about the Earthling environment, the ravages of the solar wind, and the possibility of other life forms on this blue, brown and green planet.

After a few years, I returned to Mars to share my findings with my friends and family. They were intrigued by my discoveries and decided to send me back to Earth to learn more. For many years I was in hiding, but now I've decided to expose myself and the secrets of the Martians.

I believe in sharing knowledge and making the cosmos a better place.
